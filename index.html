MY JOURNEY

I believe the most authentic and powerful experiences in life are not sought after, but rather are woven silently in your plan universe yours.

This is how I felt my journey began.

Let me introduce myself! I'm Melanie Mencarelli, a designer turned holistic health coach, lover and seeker. I journey along with other brave souls that are ready to transform their lives by releasing fear, anxiety and patterns that no longer serve them.

How did this come about?

I moved to NYC in 2010 and found myself surrounded by some of the most ambitious, self-sacrificing driven people in the world, yet when I looked into people's eyes on the street I saw that they were dull, lifeless and empty. Distracted. Focused on the future. Decisions. Auditions. Deals. Clients.

I started to truly understand that money, status, looks, things...meant nothing. People were, rather I was chasing the wrong things! I felt overjoyed at this idea, but equally detached from my environment. There was no community around me that I could talk to about my burning questions and personal revalations.

I felt lost. Alone. For a very long time. Fast forward to the present. I've broken who I thought I was down completely and rebuilt myself from the ground up. This time, my building blocks are filled with love, laughter and gratitude. It wasn't easy, but I'm here because of all the uncomfortable, terrifying situations I've been through.

I realized that I was loved. That I am love. I discovered that what brings me the most pure joy and touches me the deepest is serving. Helping others see and feel their light. Helping others out of the despair I so strongly felt myself.

It is my greatest desire to help you blossom.

I've learned that human connection is the sweetest gift we've been given. That love is infused within us and that call can be dulled, but will always be calling softly to you and that you just have to be still enough to hear it.

So find the strength within yourself to listen. Come on this journey with me.
